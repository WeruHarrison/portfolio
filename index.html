About Harrison Weru
Data Analyst

I’m Harrison Weru, a Kenyan data analyst dedicated to transforming raw data into meaningful stories that drive decisions. Over the past few years I've developed an extensive skill set to prepare for a future career in the tech industry. In particular I've had professional training in data science, project management, and technical writing. With a background in Economics and Statistics, I use data visualization, statistical methods, and machine learning to get insights out of data.

My love for data analysis started at Mount Kenya University where I took an econometrics class. It inspired me to start learning advanced excel, SPSS, Stata and R because of their capabilities to perform complex regression modelling at a faster and more accurate way. However, the data landscape is changing. AI and cloud computing are transforming the way data is accessed, transformed and analysed. Therefore, I have taken a deliberate step to enroll in Oracle's Data and AI learning paths to ensure my skills remain relevant.

In my GitHub profile, you can find the projects I have completed to demonstrate the application of different methods in practice. Connect with me to discuss the projects, potential collaborations or anything else.
